Who We Are

UMAMTEK is a Bhagalpur-based home and technical service platform founded in 2026. Our goal is to make essential services easier, faster and more reliable for every customer. From electrician, plumber, AC technician and CCTV support to general installation and repair work, UMAMTEK works as a single platform where customers can book trusted workers at their doorstep.

What Makes Us Different

UMAMTEK is not just a worker-finding platform. We focus on managed service experience, transparent pricing, booking history, technician assignment, live tracking, invoice support and customer-first communication. Customers do not need to worry about finding the right worker — they can describe their work, and UMAMTEK helps connect them with the suitable service partner.

Worker Value

Workers are the backbone of UMAMTEK. We respect skilled hands, field experience and honest labour. Our platform is designed to provide local workers better visibility, digital work opportunities, professional identity and a trusted relationship with customers. UMAMTEK believes that when workers grow, customers receive better service and the local economy becomes stronger.

Founder & CEO

Founder & CEO

M.S. Umam

UMAMTEK was founded by M.S. Umam in 2026 with a clear vision to transform the way people hire local workers and technical service providers. He believes that every customer deserves quick, professional and transparent service, and every skilled worker deserves dignity, recognition and better earning opportunities.

His aim is to build UMAMTEK as a trusted local service brand where customers can book workers for electrical, plumbing, AC, CCTV, repair, installation and maintenance work without confusion or uncertainty. Instead of depending on random contacts, UMAMTEK provides a more organized system with booking records, technician assignment, live tracking, bill details and customer support.

M.S. Umam’s long-term mission is to create a professional service ecosystem for Bhagalpur and nearby areas, where technology and local skilled workers come together. His thought is simple: “Service should be easy for customers and respectful for workers.” Through UMAMTEK, he wants to build a platform that grows with trust, responsibility and real customer satisfaction.

“A strong service company is not built only by technology, but by trust, skilled workers and honest support.”

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers for customers, workers and partners.

What is UMAMTEK?

UMAMTEK is a service provider platform where customers can book skilled workers for home, office and commercial services such as electrician, plumber, AC technician, CCTV work, repair, installation and other maintenance needs.

How can we hire workers on UMAMTEK?

Customers can visit umamtek.com, choose the worker category, select the required work, add live location and submit a booking request. After that, UMAMTEK assigns a suitable technician or worker for the service.

What kind of workers are available on UMAMTEK?

UMAMTEK plans to provide electricians, plumbers, AC technicians, CCTV technicians, carpenters, painters, masons, labour helpers, welders, supervisors, engineers, ITI technicians and general installation workers.

How does UMAMTEK ensure the quality and authenticity of workers?

UMAMTEK focuses on registering worker details, skill category, phone number, work history, customer rating and service status. As the platform grows, worker verification, rating system, completed job history and customer feedback will help improve quality and authenticity.
