Who We Are

UMAMTEK is a Bhagalpur-based service provider platform focused on making local home and technical services easier, faster and more reliable. We help customers book skilled workers for electrical work, plumbing, AC service, CCTV installation, repair, maintenance and other essential services.

Founded in 2026

UMAMTEK was founded in 2026 with a simple vision: to organize local service workers and give customers a clean, transparent and professional way to get work done at their doorstep.

Founder & CEO

UMAMTEK is founded by M.S. Umam, with the goal of building a trusted local service brand from Bhagalpur. His focus is to create a platform where customers get reliable service and workers get better digital earning opportunities.



About UMAMTEK

UMAMTEK is a modern service provider company founded in 2026 by M.S. Umam with the mission of making local home and technical services more trusted, transparent and accessible. The brand works on the idea that customers should not struggle to find the right worker; instead, they should be able to describe their problem, book a service and get connected with a suitable worker through one platform. UMAMTEK focuses on electrical services, plumbing, AC service, CCTV, installation, repair, maintenance and other essential work categories. Our tagline, “Your Project Our Priority”, represents our commitment that every customer’s work matters, whether it is a small home repair or a bigger commercial project. We also value local workers by giving them a professional platform, digital visibility and better earning opportunities. UMAMTEK aims to become a trusted bridge between customers and skilled workers, where service quality, fair pricing, live tracking, invoice records and customer support create a professional service experience.



Our Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, affordable and professional doorstep services through verified and skilled local workers. We want to reduce customer confusion, improve worker opportunities and create a smooth service experience from booking to completion.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make UMAMTEK a trusted local service platform where people can book workers for home, shop, office and commercial needs with confidence, transparency and convenience.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is UMAMTEK?

UMAMTEK is a service provider platform where customers can book skilled workers for electrical, plumbing, AC, CCTV, repair, installation and maintenance services.

How can we hire workers on UMAMTEK?

You can visit umamtek.com, choose the required worker category, select your work, add your location and submit a booking request. UMAMTEK will connect you with the suitable worker.

What kind of workers are available on UMAMTEK?

UMAMTEK aims to provide electricians, plumbers, AC technicians, CCTV technicians, carpenters, painters, masons, labour workers, welders, supervisors, engineers and general installers.

How does UMAMTEK ensure quality and authenticity?

UMAMTEK maintains worker details, assigned job records, customer feedback, service history and rating system. Over time, workers will be verified and monitored through customer reviews and performance records.

Is UMAMTEK only for electrical work?

No. UMAMTEK started with electrical services, but it is being developed as a multi-category service platform for home, office, shop and commercial needs.

What does “Your Project Our Priority” mean?

It means that every customer’s work is important for UMAMTEK. Whether it is a small repair or a complete project, we aim to handle it with responsibility and priority.
