How UMAMTEK Works

Book trusted local services in a few simple steps.

1. Select Your Worker

Choose electrician, plumber, AC technician, carpenter, painter, mason, labour, installer or other worker category.

2. Select Your Work

Search and select your exact work. If your work is not listed, choose “Other” and describe your requirement.

3. View Estimated Bill

See real rate, UMAMTEK offer rate, travel charge and total payable before submitting your booking.

4. Add Live Location

Add your live location so the service request can be processed with proper distance and tracking details.

5. Technician Assigned

UMAMTEK admin or official team assigns a suitable worker based on skill, category and availability.

6. Track & Complete

Track service status, technician ETA and get invoice after payment and work completion.
