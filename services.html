How UMAMTEK Works

Simple booking process designed for customer convenience and transparent service experience.

1

Select Worker

Choose electrician, plumber, AC technician, carpenter, painter or another worker category.

2

Select Work

Search and select your required service. If not listed, choose “Other” and describe your work.

3

Share Location

Add your live location so UMAMTEK can calculate distance, travel charge and technician route.

4

Get Service

Assigned technician visits your location, completes work and updates job status.
