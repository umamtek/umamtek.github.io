Our Projects

UMAMTEK works with the vision of providing reliable electrical, technical and maintenance solutions for homes, shops, offices, hospitals and commercial spaces across Bhagalpur.

“Every project is not just work for us — it is responsibility, trust and long-term service commitment.”

🏠 Residential Electrical Work

Complete house wiring, switch board setup, MCB installation, earthing, fan/light point installation, inverter connection and electrical fault repair for homes and apartments.

Ideal for: New homes, renovation, repair and maintenance.

🏢 Office Electrical Setup

Professional wiring, power point setup, lighting arrangement, CCTV support, backup power planning and routine electrical maintenance for office spaces.

Ideal for: Small offices, startups, coaching centers and commercial rooms.

🏪 Shop & Commercial Wiring

Electrical setup for shops, counters, lighting, display boards, plug points, CCTV wiring and load distribution for commercial use.

Ideal for: Retail shops, showrooms, restaurants and service centers.

🏥 Hospital & Clinic Maintenance

Electrical maintenance support for clinics, medical stores, small hospitals and healthcare spaces where reliable power and quick service are important.

Ideal for: Clinics, medical facilities and healthcare units.

📹 CCTV & Security Setup

CCTV camera installation, DVR/NVR support, cable routing, power supply checking and basic troubleshooting for security systems.

Ideal for: Homes, shops, offices, warehouses and schools.

🛠️ Repair & Maintenance Visits

Quick technician visits for minor repairs, fault checking, installation support, plumbing issues, AC support and general maintenance work.

Ideal for: Daily service needs and urgent repair support.



Project Approach

1. Site Visit

Our team understands the requirement, location, work type and customer expectations.

2. Work Planning

We plan worker requirement, material need, estimated time and service process.

3. Skilled Execution

Assigned workers complete the task with focus on safety, quality and customer satisfaction.

4. Support & Record

Customer gets booking status, invoice, service history and support for future reference.
